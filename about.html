About

My Story

Hello, my name is Izabela Kokoszka and I am a graphic Designer turned UX Designer. With my background as a designer I am used to working with clients and being able to work and find out together what kind of design they need. It’s very rewarding for me to take what a client has instructed to complete and giving them a piece that’s everything they wanted and more.

Design is always changing and evolving and I am very flexible and will adjust to fit the project or place I am working with. I have worked in corporate environments where there are strict guidelines and styles to follow for everything created to match the company. On the other side as well as small family businesses where there are no guidelines to follow and each project is used on the clients preferences and my own creative freedom.

As a UX Designer I am hoping to bring people and ideas together and make technology easier and more natural for everyone to use and experience.
